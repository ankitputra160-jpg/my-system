About University

Prof. Rajendra Singh (Rajju Bhaiya) University, Prayagraj is a prominent state university located in Naini, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh. Formerly known as Allahabad State University, the university is NAAC accredited with "A" Grade. It offers a wide range of Undergraduate, Postgraduate, Diploma, and Research programmes across multiple disciplines including Arts, Science, Commerce, Law, and Management.
